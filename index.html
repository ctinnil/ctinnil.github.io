QIR

Quantum Computing Simulation Research

QIR (Quantum Inspired Runtime) is an innovative platform that brings quantum computing principles to classical systems. This framework allows researchers and developers to simulate quantum algorithms, explore quantum-inspired optimization techniques, and bridge the gap between quantum theory and practical applications. QIR provides a vital testing ground for quantum concepts without requiring access to actual quantum hardware. Explore QIR
